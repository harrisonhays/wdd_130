Overview

Purpose

My website is designed to be a source information to readers about my novels. It provides information about the author (me), synopses on my books, a place to purchase said books either in physical or digital copy, and news/updates about signings I'll be at as well as books that I am currently working on.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is those who read my books or are looking to learn more about the author (me). My target audience for these novels is YA (young adult) fantasy/sci-fi/urban-fantasy readers in the age range of 14-30.

Content

Home page

Harrison Hays is an American author and business person. He discovered a passionate love-hate relationship for writing during his time in Germany as he was serving as a missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. While he would periodically write in sporadic spurts, he never tried to give writing a go as a full time career as he was afraid of not being able to support a family on the income at that time. Harrison studied Exercise Physiology at Brigham Young University Idaho for 3 semesters to become a personal trainer until realizing he didn’t need a degree for that, at which he dropped out. After spending a few years bouncing between jobs and training on the side, he realized that his current route would not land him where he wanted to ultimately be in life. After deciding to start a business and said business tanking horribly, he decided to return to school to study and eventually gained a degree in Computer Information Technology. It was there that he met the love of his life [blank] and got married shortly before graduating. After graduation Harrison spent seven years working various jobs in the IT field before retiring through an aggressive rental real estate acquisition plan that he and his wife [blank] had put together. Upon becoming bored in his early retirement, Harrison decided to pick up writing again more seriously, publishing “The Redlands”, his first book in the The Blood Works trilogy. It was an instant success to which he acredits his wife’s amazing skills with marketing. He has since finished the series and has begun working on other projects. (End of bio).
