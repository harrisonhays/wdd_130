My Books

The Blood Works

The Blood Works is a fantasy/sci-fi series written in a distant future long after nuclear warfare and biologically created kaiju wiped civilization off the face of the planet. An offshoot of humanity survived ,but not without some... changes. In a society where the strength of one's blood determines standing, those ambitious few born with less will do anything it takes to claw their way to the top, even if it means siphoning the blood from those of power. One such young man of strength is forced to watch hisonly surviving family get kindapped before his very eyes. Join Brelton as he goes on a journey to save his grandfather and ends up saving the world along the way.

The Vampire's Thrall

What does a suiciadally depressed person do when their entire family dies in a car wreck? They kill themselves of course. Or at least that's what Joshua tried to do when the crush of his life showed up on his doorstep and asked for a taste of his blood before he went. Little did either of them know that a certain pact would be formed that night stronger than any contract. Follow the story of Joshua and Victoria as they get dragged into the world of the supernatural, fighting to keep peace in a hidden society on the brink of collapse, all while trying to navigate their new relationship.

Space Odyssey

When two government sanctioned assassins take a detour mission to scrounge up some pocket change, they run into their most fearsome foe yet, fatherhood. Join Samson, Lee, their new daughter Sandra, and their sentient, satirical, ship as they travel the galaxy seeking their fortune and to right the wrongs of their upbringing.
